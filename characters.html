Sophie Hatter

Protagonist / Hatter
  • Role: Protagonist / Hatter
  • Traits: Hardworking, compassionate, stubborn, kind
  • Interesting Fact: She has the magical ability to talk life into objects, though she does not realize it at first.

Wizard Howl

Wizard / Master of the Castle
  • Role: Wizard / Master of the Castle
  • Traits: Vain, charismatic, cowardly, immensely powerful
  • Interesting Fact: In his youth, he caught a falling star (Calcifer) and gave him his heart to keep the demon alive.

Calcifer

Fire Demon / Heart of the Castle
  • Role: Fire Demon / Heart of the Castle
  • Traits: Sarcastic, loyal, fiery, powerful
  • Interesting Fact: He fuels the moving castle and heats the baths. If Howl dies, Calcifer dies as well, and vice-versa.

Markl

Wizard's Apprentice
  • Role: Wizard's Apprentice
  • Traits: Responsible, eager, friendly, playful
  • Interesting Fact: He manages daily chores and runs errands for the townspeople in Porthaven.

Witch of the Waste

Former Lover / Witch
  • Role: Former Lover / Witch
  • Traits: Possessive, powerful, bitter, later childlike
  • Interesting Fact: Banished to the Waste fifty years ago, she is obsessed with stealing Howl's heart and youth.
