Step Inside the Magic: The World of Howl's Moving Castle

From Diana Wynne Jones's beloved novel to Hayao Miyazaki's legendary film.

A Tale of Magic and Determination

The story follows a young woman named Sophie Hatter, who is cursed by the wicked Witch of the Waste and transformed into a ninety-year-old woman. Sophie sets off into the wilderness where she encounters Howl's Moving Castle – a mechanical and magical marvel of iron, steam, and secrets.

Inside the castle, she strikes a bargain with Calcifer, the fire demon who powers the colossal hearth, and meets the vain but brilliant Wizard Howl. Alongside Howl's young apprentice Markl and a strange scarecrow named Turnip Head, she finds adventure amidst an active war.

Student note

Why I chose this film

I chose Howl's Moving Castle because I like movies from Studio Ghibli, and this movie feels unique in its own world. I also like its atmosphere, characters and visual style, so it gave me enough material for a gallery, character cards and a small interactive navigation idea.
